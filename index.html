Boll Game

Instructions for Game

  • Use the arrow keys to move the ship.
  • Use space key to fire laser.
  • When laser hits the boll, boll gets damaged and your score will increase.
  • If bolls reach to the end, you will lose.
  • If ship hits the boll, your life will decrease by 1.
  • You will get 3 lifes.
  • If any glitch occurs in the game, just refresh the screen.

Press ENTER key to start the game. . . .
